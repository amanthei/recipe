Grammy Alyce Liptock's recipe. I can remember making these with her every Christmas and I carried the tradition on with my sons.
  • 1 c.
    Butter
  • 1/2 c.
    Sifted confectioner's sugar
  • 1 tsp.
    Vanilla
  • 2 1/4 c.
    Sifted flour
  • 1/4 tsp.
    Salt
  • 3/4 c.
    Finely chopped nuts

Mix butter, sugar & vanilla through. Stir flour & salt together. Blend 1/3 of the flour into butter mixture. With hands, work in remaining flour & nuts in 2 parts until dough holds together. Form dough into 1 in. balls. Place on ungreased baking sheet & bake at 400Â° for 10-12 mins. While warm, roll in confectioners sugar. Cool & roll again.
